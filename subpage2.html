Neta Gilon

is an Organizational Psychologist and a senior tech executive. For the past 20 years Netta has worked both as an HR and operations professional, and as an advisor to multiple companies. In her most recent position Netta was Chief Operating Officer at Playtika, and was part of the management team which led it to a $4.5B acquisition in 2016 by Chinese consortium “Giant”. For the past 1.5 years Netta has been consulting companies at different stages, from early stage startups to fast-growing companies. In 2014 Netta was selected for the “40 under 40” project, honoring the 40 most promising young professionals in the Israeli business world.
