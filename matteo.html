Matteo A. Legler

Is a seasoned entrepreneur and investor. Matteo started is career in a Swiss Private Bank. In 2000 he built a diversified group of companies operating in insurance brokerage , trading and wealth and asset management. Over the years, he has made significant investment in several ventures including in telecommunication (GTN) that lead to an seed stage investment in Echovox (parent Company of Zong bought for $240m by Ebay in 2011).

In 2009 he co-founded and managed an asset management Company based in Malta with representation in Switzerland and the UAE. In 2012, Matteo became COO of ADS Securties LLC an Abu Dhabi based financial firm with a paid capital of 580m USD, where he built the wealth and asset management divisions, co-headed the investment banking and led the growth of the company in Asia. Matteo has served as BOD on many Companies including regulated financial institutions in Europe, the UAE and Asia.
